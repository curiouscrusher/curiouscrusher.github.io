Road Rules

Road Rules is my solution to help solve some of the everyday problems that motorists and bikers face in modern cities. The idea is to provide basic training for proper road ettiquete to help ease tension between bicycles and cars. The app will be home to a host of information ranging from everyday tips, to your local bicycle laws in your city. While this is currently a work in progress, I aim to continue building this out and eventually produce and ship it commercially.
