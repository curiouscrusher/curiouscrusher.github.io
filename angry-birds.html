Angry Birds Packaging Concept

I developed this packaging concept for my Production Design class. The objective was to take a product that either didn't have packaging, or had terrible packaging, and develop proper packaging. I chose Angry Birds plush toys, which are typically sold on display shelves, with just a small label. I designed the packages to be able to grow as the bird arsenal expanded, with basic info about each bird and a brief bio on the back. The overall cover/texture will change depending on which bird is inside, and the die cut allows them to see out, and also helps to draw in consumers.
