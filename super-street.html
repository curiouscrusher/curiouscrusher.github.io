Super Street Re-Design

This magazine rebranding is from a school project, where our objective was to find a poorly designed magazine, and give it a fresh look. Including a new logo, cover, contents page, and a 3 page article. I chose to work with Super Street as it's a magazine I've been known to read from time to time, and it looks freadful. I started at the cover and stripped away all the unnecessary images and extra text Super Street often places there, and focused on what's important - the cars. My article spreads are designed to show off the beautiful car shots, while still allowing the type to be read easily.
